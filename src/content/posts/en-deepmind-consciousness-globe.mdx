The part of the DeepMind consciousness paper that interested me most was not the answer but the framing.

If we build a globe, it does not become Earth.

A model can represent something with surprising accuracy without becoming the thing it represents. That is the useful move here: the argument shifts from behavior to ontology.

You can disagree with the conclusion and still keep the distinction. A precise map and the territory are not the same object.
