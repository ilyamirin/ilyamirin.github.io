I do not think of AI as a layer you bolt onto a product.

The interesting work starts when models, tools, interfaces, operators, and verification rules become one coherent system. That is why my public work looks broad on the surface. It includes coding agents, media pipelines, product experiments, and infrastructure. Underneath, it is one theme: building technical worlds that hold together.

I also care about visual authorship. Good systems do not need to be visually generic. A strong interface can make complex technical behavior easier to trust, steer, and understand.

The point is not style for style's sake. It is clarity with character.
