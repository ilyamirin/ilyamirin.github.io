I am a broad AI builder with strong systems instincts and an unusually product-minded eye.

My work spans coding agents, evaluation harnesses, OpenAI-compatible infrastructure, runtime control planes, autonomous coding workflows, and generative media pipelines. The common thread is operational clarity. I care about turning model capability into systems that survive real users, real constraints, and real verification.

## What I am strongest at

- Designing AI systems that connect model behavior to usable operator workflows
- Building infrastructure and control surfaces around LLMs instead of treating them as isolated APIs
- Moving quickly from prototype energy to production-minded structure
- Keeping creative and generative work tied to systems quality instead of splitting it into a “serious work” side and a “fun side project” side

- `agent-bench-lab`: benchmark harness for end-to-end coding-agent comparison
- `aquarium_nullclaw`: control plane for isolated AI runtimes
- `replicate_proxy`: OpenAI-compatible orchestration layer for multi-provider model access
- `autocoder` and `LoomPilot`: autonomous coding delivery systems
- `cashflow-underwriting-demo`: applied ML and business-facing decision support

## Current target

Strong international roles where AI systems, product judgment, and implementation depth all need to matter at the same time. The sweet spot is work that wants both engineering rigor and enough taste to avoid building another anonymous dashboard for anxious droids.
