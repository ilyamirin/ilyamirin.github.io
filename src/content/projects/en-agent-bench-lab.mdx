This project exists because many coding-agent comparisons still stop at screenshots, demos, or intuition.

`agent-bench-lab` forces the comparison into one environment, one task, one prompt family, and one acceptance path. That makes “looks plausible” much less useful as a success signal.

What it proves about me:

- I care about evaluation, not just generation
- I can design verification around real engineering closure
- I can turn an abstract question about agents into an actual executable system
