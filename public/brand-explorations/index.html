Event Horizon Logo Concepts

Three contrast-first variants where the black hole still reads at favicon size. The main trick is simple: the core cannot share too much darkness with the tile background, so each option creates a small separation halo or lensing field around it.

Concept 1

Core Halo

  • Dark core, but with a cooler separation halo behind it
  • Most balanced candidate for logo and favicon
  • Reads clearly without looking busy
Mood: premium, legible, controlled
Concept 2

Lensed Core

  • Most physically “black-hole-like” of the set
  • The lens shape gives the core its own field
  • More celestial, slightly less minimal
Mood: optical, gravitational, elegant
Concept 3

Bright Horizon

  • Strongest contrast and brightest event horizon
  • Best if you want the favicon to pop harder
  • Least subtle, most visible
Mood: bold, visible, iconic
